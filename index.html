What Is Impact Builds?

WHAT IS
IMPACT BUILDS?

Impact Builds is a 3D single-player building game that challenges you to design, construct, and defend communities against the full force of natural disasters.

Every road you lay, every building you erect, and every policy you implement carries real consequence. Experience how infrastructure planning shapes the difference between a community that survives and one that doesn't.

Developed as a capstone academic project, Impact Builds bridges game-based learning and genuine disaster preparedness research.
